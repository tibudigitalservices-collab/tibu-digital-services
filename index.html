Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers about what TIBU does, who we help and how our process works.

What does TIBU Digital Services do? add

TIBU Digital Services builds digital infrastructure for businesses in the United States. This includes high-performance websites, private business systems, process automation, technical SEO, AI search readiness and bilingual English-Spanish digital support.

Is TIBU a marketing agency or a technology company? add

TIBU is closer to a digital engineering and business growth partner than a traditional marketing agency. We focus on websites, systems, automation, search structure and operational clarity.

Do you build websites only, or also private systems? add

We build both. TIBU creates public websites as well as private portals, internal dashboards, document areas, workflow tools and business systems that help teams organize operations.

Can TIBU help businesses in the United States? add

Yes. TIBU is positioned for small and mid-sized businesses in the United States, especially companies that need a clearer online presence, better digital operations or bilingual English-Spanish support.

Are your websites optimized for Google and AI search? add

Yes. We structure websites with semantic HTML, clear headings, helpful content, service clarity, internal linking and Schema.org where appropriate. We do not guarantee rankings, but we build a stronger technical and content foundation for search visibility.

Do you provide bilingual English and Spanish support? add

Yes. TIBU supports businesses that serve English and Spanish-speaking customers through bilingual communication, content support and digital presence planning.

Do you work with sensitive or private projects? add

Yes. TIBU has experience with private portals and sensitive digital projects. We discuss requirements carefully during consultation and avoid exposing confidential client information publicly.

How does the consultation process work? add

The process starts with a consultation to understand your business, goals, audience, current tools and digital needs. After that, TIBU recommends the right website, system, automation or SEO structure before work begins.
